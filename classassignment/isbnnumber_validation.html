About ISBN

  • ISBN-10: 10 digits format (older books)
  • ISBN-13: 13 digits format (current standard)
  • Can include hyphens or spaces (they're ignored)
  • Last digit is a checksum for validation

Valid Examples:

ISBN-13: 978-0-306-40615-7
ISBN-13: 9780306406157
ISBN-10: 0-306-40615-2
ISBN-10: 0306406152
